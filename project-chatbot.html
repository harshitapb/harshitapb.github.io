What is Genie?

Genie is a chatbot that acts as an assistant. It helps you to know about an organization, search for the details of an employee, book the conference room, fill the timesheet remotely, know the weather details, driving directions to an organization and to know the appropriate blood donors in case of emergency.

Logo Design

The logo is designed to effectively focus on the simple concept of automated assistance to make it clean and functional. Illustrator is used to designing the logo.
